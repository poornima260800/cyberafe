1.What is cyber security?

Cyber security is one of the most emerging technologies. The rapid increase in this is due to enormous amounts of data available on the internet. Any malfunction in data can lead to extraordinary impacts. Regular check-ups is made essential to ensure that the data is free from threats.

2.The CIA triad

Cyber security broadly involves both computer and information security.It involves prevention of attacks and reduced exposure to threats by reassuring that the vulnerabilty is resolved then and there. This involves two factors like

  • functionality
  • security

Increased functionality does not make it so secure and vice versa. It should strike a balance between the two ensuring smooth user access.The industry works under a CIA triad which is a standard for maintaining the data. The CIA is nothing but Confidentiality, Integrity and Availability

Details about cyber intelligence

Confidentiality means protecting the access from unauthorized users and letting only the valid users to access. Integrity is enabling both functionality and security.Availability makes the data available for the endusers.

3.Risk management

Risk management involves 5 jargons namely,

  1. Assets
  2. Vulnerability
  3. Risk
  4. Threat actor
  5. Threat

There are three kind of hackers

  • White hat hackers are one who does legally with the consent of all the parties involved
  • Black hat hackers perform unethical practices mainly to create a situation of unrest.
  • Gray hat hackers detect and perform vulnerabilties with no intentions.

4.Who are threat actors?

In this, lets us discuss threat actor in detail. Threat actor is someone who performs the threats. Threats are caused because of the exposure of vulnerability in the produced system.They may cause damage both server level and economical level. They send random crashes and worms to the well-functioning system.There are several kind of threat actors which are given in the table.

Type of threat actor Their role
Hactivists Hacker+Activist=Hackivist. These kind of actors perform threat with the agenda like political, economical or psychological one.These attacks are often funded by some organizations with the intent of defaming someone.
Hackers The main role of the hackers is to create threats.
Script Kiddies Script kiddies are one who perform threats unknowingly without their knowledge with no intention.
Injections Injections are attacks on the openly available data.
Competitors Competitors are persons trying to hack into their competitors space by stealing the confidential data there.
Insiders Insider attacks occur in an organization where the person with central authority conspires against them.
